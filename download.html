  • McGill now a Fair Trade campus

    This summer, McGill was the first university in Quebec to receive official designation as a Fair Trade campus from Fairtrade Canada. By the end of September, all coffee sold by University- or student-run stores, vending machines, residences and cafeterias will be Fair Trade certified.

Department and University Information

McGill University

Our 11 faculties and 11 professional schools offer more than 300 programs to some 38,000 graduate, undergraduate and continuing studies students.

McGill ranks 1st in Canada among medical-doctoral universities (Maclean’s) and 21st in the world (QS World University Rankings). In 2012, the University was again one of Canada’s Top 100 Employers.
